Background

I'm Darrel Tenter, founder of DLT Holdings LLC. I've spent more than 25 years working at the intersection of education, technology, and the organizations that deliver both — first as an independent consultant, then inside companies like Moodle where I managed enterprise accounts worth millions in annual revenue, and now increasingly as an operator helping organizations scale what they've built.

My background is unusually broad for this space. I've done eLearning design, LMS architecture, enterprise sales, team leadership, compliance strategy, and national expansion planning — often in the same year. That breadth isn't accidental. It's what lets me sit across from a client and understand their problem from multiple angles at once.

Current Focus

In addition to my consulting work, I currently serve as Director of Operations & Expansion at Direct Support Learning, where I'm leading the transformation of a specialized Direct Support Professional training provider from a single-state Oregon operation into a national platform. The work involves standardizing delivery models, building multi-state compliance infrastructure, and optimizing the LMS and CRM stack to support enrollment and reporting at national scale.

It's exactly the kind of problem I find most interesting: taking something that works well in one context and engineering it to work everywhere.

How I Work

I work with a small number of clients at any given time, which means the people I partner with get my full attention — not a junior associate's. I ask a lot of questions before I offer opinions, because the presenting problem is rarely the actual problem. And I try to leave every engagement with the organization more capable than I found it.

I'm based in Helena, Montana and work remotely with clients across the country. For the right engagement, I'm available for on-site work as well.
